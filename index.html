About Me

Crafting Sound Stories Since 2014

I'm a passionate music producer and sound engineer based in Moscow, with over a decade of experience shaping audio landscapes across multiple genres. My journey began in electronic music production, eventually expanding into film scoring, game audio, and commercial sound design.

My approach combines technical precision with creative intuition, ensuring every project achieves its full sonic potential.
